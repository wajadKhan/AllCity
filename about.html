Multi-investor real estate acquisitions are known as syndications. They have built in protections for all parties.

Our deals are structured our as a Limited Liability Company with the Sponsor participating as the General Partner and the investors participating as limited partners / passive members.

The rights and responsibilities of each party are set forth in the LLC Operating Agreement.
